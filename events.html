Signature event — ByronFest

Annual Festival  ·  July 10–12, 2026

ByronFest 39

Thursday – Saturday  ·  Downtown Byron, IL

Byron's biggest event of the year and one of the Midwest's premier community festivals. Three days of live music on multiple stages, carnival rides, food vendors, the famous Bags Tournament, and thousands of visitors from across the region. ByronFest 39 is a milestone — and the Chamber is bringing it in style.
