Welcome to Byron, Illinois

Welcome Ogle County Quality of Life Economic Development Tourism Organizations

Welcome to Byron, Illinois — a thriving community nestled along the Rock River in Ogle County. Known as the "Gateway to the Rock River," Byron offers small-town charm with big-community heart. Whether you're a visitor, a prospective resident, or a business looking to grow, you'll find a warm and welcoming place here.

A community worth knowing

Byron is home to excellent schools, beautiful parks, a vibrant downtown, and events that draw visitors from across northern Illinois and beyond. ByronFest alone draws thousands of attendees each July to the banks of the Rock River.

The Byron Area Chamber of Commerce has been serving the community since 1958 — connecting businesses, advocating for growth, and celebrating the people and organizations that make Byron a great place to live, work, and visit.

Getting here

Byron is located along Illinois Route 2 and Route 72, approximately 20 miles west of Rockford and 100 miles west of Chicago. The Rock River runs through the heart of town, making it a scenic destination year-round.
