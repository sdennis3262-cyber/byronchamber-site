Economic Development

Welcome Ogle County Quality of Life Economic Development Tourism Organizations

Byron's economy is diverse, stable, and growing. Anchored by energy production, manufacturing, healthcare, and a vibrant local business community, Byron offers a strong economic foundation for businesses of all sizes.

Energy

Constellation Energy's Byron Generating Station is one of the region's largest employers and a cornerstone of the local tax base.

Manufacturing

Byron is home to precision manufacturers including Armor Coated Technology, Loomis International, and National Header Die.

Healthcare

A growing healthcare sector with OSF Medical Group, UW Health, Mercyhealth, and multiple specialty and therapy providers.

Retail & Dining

A walkable downtown with locally-owned restaurants, bars, and shops contributing to a vibrant commercial district.

Financial Services

Multiple banks, credit services, insurance, and financial planning firms serving residents and businesses alike.

Agriculture

The surrounding Ogle County region supports robust agricultural production, contributing to the regional economy and food supply.

Why businesses choose Byron

Byron's location along Illinois Route 2 and Route 72 provides easy access to Rockford, Dixon, and the greater I-90 corridor. The community offers affordable commercial real estate, a skilled workforce, low crime, and a customer base that includes over 11,000 residents in the greater Byron area.

The Byron Area Chamber of Commerce actively advocates for pro-business policies and connects new businesses with resources, networking, and community visibility from day one.

Start or grow your business in Byron

The Chamber works alongside the Ogle County Economic Development Corporation and local government to support new business development, ribbon cuttings, and ongoing member success. Whether you're opening your first location or expanding an existing operation, Byron wants to see you succeed.
