Community Organizations

Welcome Ogle County Quality of Life Economic Development Tourism Organizations

Byron's strength comes from its people and the organizations that bring them together. From civic clubs to faith communities to non-profits serving the most vulnerable — there's no shortage of ways to get involved, give back, and connect in Byron.

Byron Area Chamber of Commerce

The Chamber connects businesses, advocates for growth, and organizes community events that bring Byron together year-round.

(815) 234-5500  |  office@byronchamber.com

Byron Lions Club

A cornerstone of the Byron community, the Lions Club serves locally and globally through fundraising, volunteer work, and community service initiatives.

(815) 871-4898

Rock River Valley Rotary

Connecting professionals and community leaders through service, networking, and a shared commitment to making Byron and the Rock River Valley better.

United Way of Ogle County

Supporting health, education, and financial stability for families throughout Ogle County through fundraising and community partnerships.

(815) 284-3339  |  unitedwayofoglecounty.org

Sinnissippi Centers, Inc.

Providing mental health, substance use, and developmental disability services to individuals and families across northern Illinois. Located at 212 W. Blackhawk Dr.

(815) 209-0998  |  sinnissippi.org

Byron Civic Theatre

Bringing live theatre and the arts to the Byron community through productions, events, and a passion for the performing arts in northern Illinois.

Byron Women's Club

A longstanding community organization dedicated to charitable giving, civic improvement, and fellowship among Byron's women leaders.

CASA — 15th Judicial Circuit

Court Appointed Special Advocates serving children in the foster care system in Ogle and surrounding counties, ensuring every child has a voice.

Faith communities

Byron is home to thirteen area churches representing a variety of Christian traditions, including Cornerstone Family Church, Saint Mary Catholic Church, United Church of Byron, Middle Creek Presbyterian Church, and others. Faith communities play an important role in Byron's social fabric and volunteer landscape.

Get involved

The best way to find your place in Byron is to show up. Chamber events, ribbon cuttings, ByronFest volunteer opportunities, and community organization meetings are all open and welcoming. Reach out to the Chamber at office@byronchamber.com and we'll help connect you with the right people.
